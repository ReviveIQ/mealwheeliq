Why we care about organic

USDA Certified Organic produce contains no synthetic pesticides or GMOs. Harvard T.H. Chan School of Public Health research links organic diets to measurably lower pesticide exposure — especially important for children. Organic livestock cannot receive antibiotics or growth hormones. The World Health Organization identifies routine antibiotic use in livestock as a primary driver of global antibiotic resistance. MealsWheel flags organic priorities on every recipe so you know exactly where to spend the extra dollar.
