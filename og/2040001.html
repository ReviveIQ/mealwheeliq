🍚 Seared Chicken Breast & Bell Pepper Rice Bowl with Lemon-Herb Greek Yogurt

30 min · Easy · Mediterranean · 641 kcal per serving

Ingredients

  • 1.75 lbs chicken breast
  • 1.5 cups white rice
  • 2 piece bell pepper
  • 1 cups cherry tomatoes
  • 3 cups spinach
  • 0.75 cups Greek yogurt
  • 1 piece lemon
  • 3 tbsp garlic-infused olive oil
  • 2 oz feta cheese
  • 3 piece scallions (green tops only)

Instructions

  1. Cook white rice in 3 cups of water with a pinch of salt according to package directions, about 15 minutes; keep warm over low heat.
  2. Slice chicken breasts horizontally into 3/4-inch-thick cutlets, then season aggressively on both sides with salt, black pepper, paprika, and oregano.
  3. Heat 2 tablespoons garlic-infused olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat until shimmering. Sear chicken cutlets in a single layer until deeply golden and cooked through, about 4 minutes per side; transfer to a cutting board and tent loosely with foil.
  4. Add remaining 1 tablespoon garlic-infused olive oil to the same skillet. Toss in sliced bell peppers and cherry tomatoes, cooking over medium-high heat until peppers are lightly charred at the edges and tomatoes begin to burst, about 4 minutes. Fold in spinach and cook, stirring, until just wilted, about 1 minute more; season with salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes.
  5. Whisk together Greek yogurt, lemon zest, 2 tablespoons lemon juice, salt, and black pepper in a small bowl until smooth and creamy.
  6. Slice rested chicken against the grain into thick strips. Divide warm rice among three bowls, top with the pepper-spinach mixture and sliced chicken, then dollop generously with lemon-herb yogurt. Garnish with crumbled feta and sliced scallion greens; serve immediately with remaining lemon wedges.
