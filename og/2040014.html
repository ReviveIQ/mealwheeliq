🥢 Ginger-Scallion Pork Chops with Stir-Fried Broccoli and Brown Rice

30 min · Medium · Chinese · 494 kcal per serving

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs Pork chops, boneless
  • 4 cups Broccoli, cut into florets
  • 1.5 cups Brown rice
  • 2 tbsp Fresh ginger, peeled and finely grated
  • 4 stalks Green onions, green tops only, thinly sliced
  • 1 large Bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 2 medium Carrots, julienned
  • 3 tbsp Garlic-infused olive oil
  • 2 tbsp Toasted sesame oil
  • 2 tbsp Low-sodium tamari (gluten-free soy sauce alternative)
  • 1 tbsp Rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp Cornstarch
  • 0.25 cup Fresh cilantro, roughly chopped
  • 0.5 tsp Red pepper flakes
  • 0.5 tsp Black pepper
  • 0.33 cup Chicken broth, low-sodium

Instructions

  1. Cook brown rice according to package directions (about 25–30 minutes for quick-cook brown rice or use a rice cooker). While rice cooks, prepare all remaining ingredients.
  2. Whisk together tamari, rice vinegar, sesame oil, cornstarch, and chicken broth in a small bowl to form the sauce. Set aside.
  3. Heat 1.5 tbsp garlic-infused olive oil in a large cast-iron skillet or wok over high heat until shimmering. Pat pork chops dry, season with black pepper, and sear undisturbed until deep golden brown, about 4 minutes per side. Transfer to a cutting board and tent with foil; let rest 5 minutes, then slice thinly against the grain.
  4. Return the skillet to high heat and add remaining 1.5 tbsp garlic-infused olive oil. Add carrots and bell pepper and stir-fry until just beginning to soften, about 2 minutes. Add broccoli florets and stir-fry until bright green and tender-crisp, about 3 minutes more.
  5. Push vegetables to the edges of the pan, add ginger and red pepper flakes to the center, and stir-fry 30 seconds until fragrant. Pour the tamari-broth sauce over everything, tossing vigorously to coat and letting it thicken, about 1–2 minutes.
  6. Return sliced pork to the pan, folding it into the vegetables and sauce to heat through, about 1 minute. Remove from heat and drizzle with any remaining sesame oil.
  7. Divide brown rice among three bowls. Top with the pork and vegetable stir-fry, garnish generously with sliced green onion tops and fresh cilantro, and serve immediately.
