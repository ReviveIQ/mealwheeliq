🥗 Seared Shrimp & Spinach Power Salad with Lemon-Ginger Vinaigrette

30 min · Easy · Mediterranean-Asian Fusion · 469 kcal per serving

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs frozen shrimp, extra-large (21-25 count), thawed, peeled, deveined
  • 6 oz fresh baby spinach
  • 2 cups brown rice, cooked
  • 1.5 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 piece cucumber, sliced into half-moons
  • 2 piece bell pepper (red or yellow), thinly sliced
  • 2 piece carrots, peeled and julienned or shaved with a vegetable peeler
  • 4 piece green onions, dark green tops only (thinly sliced)
  • 0.5 cups fresh cilantro, roughly chopped
  • 0.25 cups fresh parsley, roughly chopped
  • 3 oz feta cheese, pasteurized, crumbled
  • 0.5 cups Greek yogurt, full-fat, plain
  • 2 piece lemon (zest and juice)
  • 1.5 tbsp fresh ginger, finely grated
  • 6 tbsp garlic-infused olive oil
  • 3 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tsp honey
  • 0.33 cups panko breadcrumbs (gluten-free)
  • 2 piece avocado, sliced

Instructions

  1. Whisk together 3 tbsp garlic-infused olive oil, extra-virgin olive oil, lemon zest, lemon juice, grated ginger, Dijon mustard, honey, and a pinch of red pepper flakes in a small bowl until emulsified. Stir in Greek yogurt until smooth, then taste and adjust acidity with more lemon juice if needed. Set the creamy lemon-ginger dressing aside.
  2. Toast panko breadcrumbs in a dry skillet over medium heat, stirring constantly until deep golden and crisp, about 3–4 minutes. Transfer immediately to a plate to cool — they will burn quickly if left in the pan.
  3. Pat shrimp completely dry with paper towels and season lightly with paprika and black pepper. Heat 3 tbsp garlic-infused olive oil in a large cast-iron or stainless skillet over high heat until shimmering. Add shrimp in a single layer and sear undisturbed until pink and lightly charred on the bottom, about 2 minutes. Flip and cook until just opaque and cooked through, 1 minute more. Transfer to a plate.
  4. Pile spinach into a large wide serving bowl. Add warm brown rice, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, bell pepper, carrots, and avocado slices. Drizzle two-thirds of the dressing over the vegetables and toss gently until the spinach begins to wilt slightly from the residual heat of the rice.
  5. Arrange seared shrimp over the salad, scatter green onion tops, cilantro, and parsley across the surface, then crumble pasteurized feta evenly over the top.
  6. Drizzle remaining dressing over the shrimp, shower with toasted panko for crunch, and serve immediately directly from the bowl — divide into three portions at the table.
