🥗 Seared Chicken & Charred Broccoli Salad with Lemon-Herb Yogurt Dressing

30 min · Medium · Mediterranean · 271 kcal per serving

Ingredients

  • 1.75 lbs chicken breast
  • 4 cups broccoli florets
  • 1.5 cups cherry tomatoes
  • 5 oz spinach
  • 3 oz feta cheese (lactose-free or sheep/goat feta)
  • 0.5 cup Greek yogurt (lactose-free)
  • 2 piece lemon
  • 0.33 cup fresh parsley
  • 0.25 cup fresh cilantro
  • 4 stalks green onions (green tops only)
  • 2 piece avocado
  • 5 tbsp garlic-infused olive oil
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tsp fresh ginger
  • 1.5 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 0.5 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 0.5 tsp salt

Instructions

  1. Prep all components: pound chicken breasts to an even 3/4-inch thickness between plastic wrap. In a small bowl, whisk together 1.5 tsp paprika, 1 tsp cumin, 0.5 tsp red pepper flakes, 0.5 tsp black pepper, and 0.25 tsp salt; rub evenly over both sides of chicken. Zest and juice both lemons, slice green onion tops thinly, and roughly chop parsley and cilantro.
  2. Roast the broccoli: Heat oven to 475°F (or use a cast-iron skillet over high heat). Toss broccoli florets with 1.5 tbsp garlic-infused olive oil and a pinch of black pepper, then spread in a single layer on a sheet pan. Roast until deeply charred at the edges and tender-crisp, about 12–14 minutes, flipping once halfway. Remove and let cool slightly.
  3. Sear the chicken: Heat 1.5 tbsp garlic-infused olive oil in a large cast-iron or stainless skillet over medium-high heat until shimmering. Add chicken and sear undisturbed until a deep golden-brown crust forms, about 5–6 minutes per side, or until an instant-read thermometer reads 165°F. Transfer to a cutting board and rest for 5 minutes, then slice against the grain into 1/2-inch strips.
  4. Whisk the dressing: In a medium bowl, combine lactose-free Greek yogurt, 2 tbsp garlic-infused olive oil, all lemon juice and zest, Dijon mustard, grated fresh ginger, and 0.25 tsp black pepper. Whisk until smooth and creamy. Fold in half the chopped parsley and cilantro. Thin with 1–2 tbsp water if needed to reach a pourable consistency.
  5. Build the salad base: Toss fresh spinach with 0.5 tbsp garlic-infused olive oil and a light squeeze of lemon in a large bowl until lightly coated. Divide across three wide serving bowls or plates. Top evenly with charred broccoli, halved cherry tomatoes, and sliced avocado.
  6. Finish and serve: Arrange sliced chicken over each salad, drizzle generously with the lemon-herb yogurt dressing, and crumble feta evenly over the top. Scatter green onion tops and remaining fresh herbs over each bowl, finish with a crack of black pepper, taste and adjust salt if needed, and serve immediately while the chicken is still warm.
