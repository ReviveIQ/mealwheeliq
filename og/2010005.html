🍳 Skillet Chicken & Broccoli Rice Bowl with Lemon-Herb Pan Sauce

30 min · Easy · American Comfort · 771 kcal per serving

Ingredients

  • 1.25 lbs Chicken Breast
  • 1.5 cups White Rice
  • 3 cups Broccoli
  • 1 cup Cherry Tomatoes
  • 2 oz Parmesan
  • 0.33 cup Greek Yogurt
  • 1 piece Lemon
  • 3 tbsp Garlic-infused olive oil
  • 2.5 cups Chicken Broth
  • 2 tbsp Butter
  • 1 tsp Paprika
  • 1 tsp Oregano
  • 0.25 tsp Red pepper flakes
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 0.5 tsp Black pepper

Instructions

  1. Bring 1.75 cups chicken broth and 0.5 cup water to a boil in a medium saucepan. Stir in white rice, reduce heat to low, cover, and cook until all liquid is absorbed and rice is tender, about 18 minutes. Remove from heat and keep covered.
  2. Slice chicken breasts horizontally into cutlets about 3/4-inch thick, then season aggressively on both sides with paprika, oregano, red pepper flakes, salt, and black pepper.
  3. Heat 2 tablespoons garlic-infused olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat until shimmering. Sear chicken cutlets until deep golden brown, about 3–4 minutes per side, then transfer to a cutting board and tent loosely with foil.
  4. Add remaining 1 tablespoon garlic-infused olive oil and 1 tablespoon butter to the same skillet over medium-high heat. Toss in broccoli florets and cherry tomatoes, stirring to coat in the pan drippings, and cook until broccoli is bright green and lightly charred and tomatoes are just beginning to blister, about 4–5 minutes.
  5. Pour in remaining 3/4 cup chicken broth, scraping up any browned bits from the bottom of the pan. Simmer until broth reduces by half and coats the vegetables, about 2–3 minutes. Remove from heat and stir in remaining 1 tablespoon butter and the juice of half the lemon.
  6. Whisk Greek yogurt with a squeeze of lemon juice, a pinch of salt, and half the Parmesan in a small bowl until smooth. Slice chicken against the grain into strips.
  7. Divide rice among three bowls, top with sliced chicken and broccoli-tomato mixture, drizzle with lemon-yogurt sauce, and finish with remaining Parmesan. Season to taste with salt and black pepper and serve immediately with remaining lemon wedges alongside.
