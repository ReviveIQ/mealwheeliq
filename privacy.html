Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 9, 2026 · Last updated: July 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Bryan Michael Greer, doing business as ReviveIQ ("we," "us," or "our"), collects, uses, and protects information when you use MealWheelIQ ("Service") at mealwheeliq.com.

We take your privacy seriously. We collect only what we need to run the Service, we do not sell your data, and we give you control over your information.

The short version: We collect your email, pantry ingredients, dietary preferences, and recipe history to power the app. We share data with Stripe (payments), Anthropic (AI recipes), Resend (email delivery), and TiDB (database). We never sell your data. You can delete your account and data at any time.

1. Information We Collect

What Why When

Pantry ingredients To generate recipes from what you actually have. Stored per account. When you add ingredients or scan a photo
Dietary preferences & allergies To ensure recipes respect your restrictions and health goals When you set preferences in onboarding or settings

Fridge/pantry photos Sent to Anthropic's API for ingredient identification. Not stored after processing. When you use photo scan
Usage data Spin counts (for plan enforcement). No browsing tracking or analytics beyond this. Ongoing

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate personalized recipes and meal plans based on your pantry and preferences
  • To maintain your account and subscription status
  • To deliver recipe results and grocery lists to your email (when requested)
  • To enforce plan limits (e.g. free tier spin counts)
  • To send transactional emails — receipts, account notices, and recipe delivery
  • To improve the Service based on aggregate, anonymized usage patterns

We do not use your data for advertising. We do not build behavioral profiles for sale. We do not send marketing emails without your explicit consent.

3. Who We Share Data With

Partner What they receive Why
Anthropic Your pantry ingredients, dietary preferences, and fridge photos (when scanning) To generate recipes using Claude AI
Stripe Email address, subscription status, payment details To process payments securely
Resend Email address and recipe content To deliver recipe emails and grocery lists

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for marketing purposes.

4. Fridge and Pantry Photos

When you use the photo scan feature, your image is compressed on your device and sent to Anthropic's API solely for ingredient identification. We do not store your photos. The image is processed and discarded — only the list of identified ingredients is saved to your account.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required by law to retain records (e.g. payment records for tax purposes, retained for 7 years per IRS requirements).

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction — update your email, preferences, or pantry at any time within the app
  • Deletion — delete your account and all associated data by contacting hello@mealwheeliq.com
  • Portability — request your recipe history and pantry data in a readable format
  • Opt-out — unsubscribe from any marketing communications at any time

7. California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act:

  • The right to know what personal information we collect and how it is used
  • The right to delete personal information we have collected from you
  • The right to opt out of the sale of personal information — we do not sell personal information
  • The right to non-discrimination for exercising your CCPA rights

To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@mealwheeliq.com with the subject line "CCPA Request."

8. Children's Privacy

MealWheelIQ is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, contact us at hello@mealwheeliq.com and we will delete it promptly.

9. Security

We protect your data using industry-standard measures:

  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt (never stored in plain text)
  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS
  • Database connections use SSL encryption
  • Authentication uses JWT tokens with expiration

No system is 100% secure. If you suspect unauthorized access to your account, contact us immediately at hello@mealwheeliq.com.

10. Cookies and Local Storage

MealWheelIQ uses browser localStorage to store your authentication token and app preferences (theme, onboarding status). We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies. We do not use Google Analytics or similar tracking tools.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the Service evolves. We will notify you of material changes by email or in-app notice. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
